WhAT IS The best Solution for you?

umbrella employment

Working through an Umbrella Employment Solution is often the easiest option. It makes your Pay As You Earn (PAYE) and National Insurance (NI) deductions for you, and allows you to maximise the amount you take home by offsetting expenses like travel, accommodation and meals against tax. Speak to one of our expert advisors to find out more about our solution.

OR

Limited Company

Provides full control for you of your own limited company and options regarding claiming costs and other tax deductions. Setting yourself up as a Shareholder and Director of a limited company allows you to draw some of your (income) funds as a dividend rather than all being paid as salary, this means your overall tax burden may be reduced.
